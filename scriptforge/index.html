About ScriptForge

Your go-to AI tool that transforms ideas into content.

ScriptForge is TAHA Studio's end-to-end AI writing and video production tool. Paste a single idea — a product, a story, a message you want people to hear — and ScriptForge writes it into a fully segmented, timed script, complete with on-camera direction, TTS-ready dialogue, B-roll prompts, camera motion, and audio notes. Then, segment by segment, it hands that script straight to Google Veo 3.1, xAI's Grok Imagine, or HeyGen's Video Agent to generate an actual video clip — voice, music, and all. No separate scriptwriting app. No separate storyboard tool. No separate video generator. One idea in, one production-ready video out.

It's built for solo creators, marketers, agencies, and small businesses who need short-form video — ads, explainers, social clips, documentary segments — without a writers' room, a studio, or a production schedule measured in weeks.

Time saved: what used to take a script draft, a revision pass, a storyboard, a voice actor booking, and a day in an edit bay now takes minutes. Paste your idea and ScriptForge formats a complete, timed script in under a minute. Click "Generate clip" on any segment, and a spoken, scored video clip is ready a few minutes later — no editing software, no timeline, no rendering rig of your own. Projects that used to take days now finish in one sitting.

What makes it different

  • All-in-one pipeline — script writing, segmenting, and video generation in one tool, not three.
  • Your keys, your cost — bring your own Veo, Grok, or HeyGen API key and pay providers directly. No markup, no locked-in credit packs.
  • Provider choice per segment — pick the best generator for each moment: Veo or Grok for silent B-roll and dialogue, HeyGen for a talking, on-camera presenter.
  • Full creative control — aspect ratio, resolution, duration, and up to three reference images to keep a character or product consistent across every clip.
  • Privacy by design — your scripts and API keys are relayed for a single request only, never stored, never logged.
  • Built for real production — every segment lands in exact 10-second blocks, matching how social platforms and ad slots actually cut.
